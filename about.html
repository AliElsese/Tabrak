Tabrak Najd

Trabak Najd believes that wise management is the foundation for continued success in any area.
From this point of view, we at Tabrak Company have an administrative and technical structure with expertise, dynamic dynamics and high efficiency in performance to meet our commitments to our valued customers.
Time management is our specialty. We strive to ensure high standards of quality within the agreed time frame.
Our goal is to meet the different requirements of all our clients, from the owner, the consultant and the subcontractors within a deep approach of transparency by handling and accuracy of execution and delivery of the works.
We learned a lot of lessons from our work experience and we want to put this expertise, skill and teamwork together with integrity, commitment and confidence to drive productivity for our projects that will be the basis of our reputation that we want to build with you and through you.
Tabrak Najd is a Saudi national company established in the city of Riyadh with a commercial registration number: 1010260949, Since its founding, the company has been actively engaged in general contracting. The company has implemented many governmental and private projects. The Ministry of Interior, Ministry of Defense, Ministry of Health and Saudi Oger are among our most important clients.
